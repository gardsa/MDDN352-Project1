Your Weather Your Way

Outline

4C is a weather application that offers users the ability to customise their own interface. The problem with current weather applications is that they don’t display the information users actually want. On the contrary, weather websites often provide too much information, and it takes users too long to understand and process what they are seeing.

4C is targeting the gap in this market - allowing users to select the weather components that matter to them. The landing screen depicts a maximum of four weather components which can be changed at any time...
