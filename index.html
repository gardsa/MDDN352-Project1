Your Weather Your Way

Personal

Are you sick of seeing weather information that's irrelevant to you? Is it doing your head in trying to understand all the graphs and numbers that don't make any sense? 4C lets you customise your home screen to display the weather information YOU want to see.

Local

4C draws from a number of local and national weather data services. You can even sync it to your BloomSkyTM weather station, giving you easy access to weather information just outside your front door.

Easy

Simple drag-drop and resize functionality allows you to reshuffle the home screen with ease. It's simple to pick what weather information you'd like to see in the Settings menu - where you can also change the colour scheme.
